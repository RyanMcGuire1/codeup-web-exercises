Andromeda Galaxy

  • Also known as Messier 31, M31, or NGC 224
  • Originally known as Andromeda Nebula
  • A barred spiral galaxy
  • Approximately 2.5 million light-years from earth

Betelgeuse

  • Usually the tenth-brightest star in the night sky
  • the second-brightest star in the constellation og Orion
  • A distinctly reddish semi-regular variable star
  • Apparent magnitude varies between +0.0 and +1.6

Ceres

  • The largest object in the main asteroid belt that lies between the orbits of Mars and Jupiter
  • Has a diameter of 940 km
  • Both the largest of the asteroids and the only dwarf planet inside Neptune's orbit
  • the 25th-largest body in the Solar System within the orbit of Neptune
